## Core value propositions
1. **Affordable** — enterprise-grade AI without enterprise cost (core positioning).
2. **Integrated & cloud-based** — one connected platform, manage every site remotely.
3. **Beyond security** — analytics that improve operations & customer engagement (heat mapping, drive-thru timing, exception reporting).
4. **Trust & service** — decades of results; "treat every customer like family."
