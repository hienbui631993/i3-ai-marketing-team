## What i3 does (one line)
Affordable, **cloud-based, integrated AI** video solutions that combine surveillance
with business insights — enhancing **safety, security, operational efficiency, and
customer engagement** for multi-site businesses (Retail, QSR, Schools, and more).

## Software & platform (the core)
| Product | What it is (verified) |
|---------|------------------------|
| **SRX-Pro** | i3's flagship video management software (VMS) — powerful surveillance + business-intelligence data, on-prem or via cloud. Manages up to 64 cameras (IP + HD analog via encoder). |
| **i3Ai** | Real-time AI object detection & tracking software; powers analytics (people counting, dwell/throughput, drive-thru timing, detection). |
| **Cloud Managed Services (CMS)** | **SOC 2 certified** cloud platform — centralized, remote access to operational insights, predictive analytics, asset protection, and safety monitoring from anywhere. The hub the solutions run on. |
| **Video Pilot Client / Matrix** | Viewing clients for live/recorded video across sites. |
| **VEO** | Video Embedded DVR. |

## Core value propositions
1. **Affordable** — enterprise-grade integrated AI without the enterprise price (core positioning).
2. **Integrated & cloud-based** — SRX-Pro + i3Ai + SOC 2 CMS; manage every site remotely.
3. **Beyond security** — analytics that improve operations & engagement (Heat Mapping, Drive-Thru Timer, Smart-ER).
4. **Trust & service** — decades of results; "treat every customer like family."

## Key verticals (verified emphasis)
- **Retail / loss prevention** — Heat Mapping, Smart-ER exception reporting, Sentry.
- **QSR (quick-service restaurants)** — Velocity Drive-Thru Timer, queue management.
- **Schools / education** — safety, monitoring, NDAA-compliant options.
- Plus grocery, convenience, multi-site enterprise.
