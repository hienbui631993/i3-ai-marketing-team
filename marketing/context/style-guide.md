## Typography
- **Primary font: Montserrat** — used across the approved deck; bold for headlines/section
  titles, regular for body. Use for primary marketing materials, decks, websites.
  *(Updated from Helvetica Neue per the deck direction.)*
- **Web fallback stack:** `"Montserrat", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif`.
- **Secondary (warmer verticals — Schools, QSR, Retail):** Gitlab Sans / Barlow, per brand kit.
- Hierarchy: bold headings, regular body, generous line height.

## Logo usage (official)
Assets in [`/i3 Media Kit/i3 Logo/`](../../i3%20Media%20Kit/i3%20Logo/). Full rules in
[`i3_Logo_Quick_Guide_2026.pdf`](../../i3%20Media%20Kit/i3%20Logo/i3_Logo_Quick_Guide_2026.pdf).

| Version | When to use | Files |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| **Primary Logo** (no tagline, ™) | Legal docs, contracts, compliance, software/product UI | `Primary Logos/i3 logo blue.(svg/png)` |
| **Marketing Logo** (with tagline "Affordable Integrated AI Solutions") | Marketing, presentations, websites, tradeshows, packaging | `Primary Logos/i3 logo taglineH/V.(svg/png)` |
| **Reversed / white** | On dark or photographic backgrounds | `Secondary Logos/…white.png`, `i3 logo white.png` |
| **Black** | One-color / mono contexts | `Secondary Logos/i3 logo black….png` |

**Rules:**
- Match logo to background — **light logos on dark, dark logos on light.** Keep clear & consistent.
- Respect the **safe zone** (clear space ≈ height of the "3" on all sides; 2× margins shown in kit).
- **Minimum size:** 1.5 inch (print) / 140 px (digital) wide. Never smaller.
- Never stretch, recolor, or crowd the logo. Apply safe zone even beside partner logos.

## Editorial formatting rules
- **Headers** phrased as the reader's question where possible (good for AI search).
- Short paragraphs (2–4 sentences); bullets over walls of text.
- **One clear CTA** per asset.
- Numbers as numerals ("3 sites", "12% shrink").
- Always include alt-text intent for images in web assets.

## Deck conventions (for Branded Deck skill)
- Follow the approved template + [`templates/branded-deck/ANALYSIS.md`](../templates/branded-deck/ANALYSIS.md).
- **Dark `#0A1428` base, magenta `#FF007D` accents, white type, Montserrat** throughout;
  16:9; full-bleed media; one idea per slide; bold/magenta the key phrases.
- Reuse the deck's narrative flow (cover → about → core applications → capability areas →
  per-vertical → partnership close). Logo (white) on the dark base.

## Social conventions
- LinkedIn: fearless hook in line 1, insight body, soft CTA, 3–5 relevant hashtags.
- Carousels: 1 idea per slide, big readable headline (Montserrat), consistent i3 template,
  dark `#0A1428` base with a single accent (magenta `#FF007D` or brand blue).
