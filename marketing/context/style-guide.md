## Brand colors (official)

### Brand Expression Colors — *for marketing, storytelling & category differentiation* (not UI functional colors)
| Name | HEX | RGB | Pantone |
|------|-----|-----|---------|
| **i3 Pink** | `#DF1E71` | 223/30/113 | 214 C |
| **i3 Green** | `#00A661` | 0/166/97 | 3405 C |
| **i3 Yellow** | `#FFC107` | 255/193/7 | 7549 C |

> Expression colors are for **gradients with brand colors, icons, infographics, and
> accent tones** — emotion and flexibility. Don't use them as primary UI colors.
> The brand kit shows a signature **blue→pink→yellow gradient** bar; use sparingly as a hero accent.

## Typography (official)
- **Primary font: Helvetica Neue** — modern, clear, professional. Use for **all primary
  marketing materials, websites, and official documents.**
- **Secondary fonts: Gitlab Sans** (and **Barlow**) — for a warmer, more approachable
  feel when designing for verticals like **Schools, QSR, and Retail.** Pair with Helvetica Neue.
- Hierarchy: bold headings, regular body, generous line height.

## Logo usage (official)
Assets in [`/i3 Media Kit/i3 Logo/`](../../i3%20Media%20Kit/i3%20Logo/). Full rules in
[`i3_Logo_Quick_Guide_2026.pdf`](../../i3%20Media%20Kit/i3%20Logo/i3_Logo_Quick_Guide_2026.pdf).

| Version | When to use | Files |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| **Primary Logo** (no tagline, ™) | Legal docs, contracts, compliance, software/product UI | `Primary Logos/i3 logo blue.(svg/png)` |
| **Marketing Logo** (with tagline "Affordable Integrated AI Solutions") | Marketing, presentations, websites, tradeshows, packaging | `Primary Logos/i3 logo taglineH/V.(svg/png)` |
| **Reversed / white** | On dark or photographic backgrounds | `Secondary Logos/…white.png`, `i3 logo white.png` |
| **Black** | One-color / mono contexts | `Secondary Logos/i3 logo black….png` |

**Rules:**
- Match logo to background — **light logos on dark, dark logos on light.** Keep clear & consistent.
- Respect the **safe zone** (clear space ≈ height of the "3" on all sides; 2× margins shown in kit).
- **Minimum size:** 1.5 inch (print) / 140 px (digital) wide. Never smaller.
- Never stretch, recolor, or crowd the logo. Apply safe zone even beside partner logos.

## Imagery
- Real-world business contexts: retail floors, QSR/drive-thru, control rooms, multi-site
  dashboards, the i3 cloud/VMS UI, AI detections in clean modern environments.
- Professional and well-lit; warm and trustworthy, **not** threatening/dystopian.
- Product & solution renders available in [`/i3 Media Kit/Product Images/`](../../i3%20Media%20Kit/Product%20Images/).

## Editorial formatting rules
- **Headers** phrased as the reader's question where possible (good for AI search).
- Short paragraphs (2–4 sentences); bullets over walls of text.
- **One clear CTA** per asset.
- Numbers as numerals ("3 sites", "12% shrink").
- Always include alt-text intent for images in web assets.

## Deck conventions (for Branded Deck skill)
- Structure: title → agenda → problem → solution → proof → offer/CTA.
- Brand Blue `#00588F` as primary; Dark Blue `#002447` for hero/section dividers;
  expression colors only as chart/accent. Helvetica Neue throughout.
- Logo on every slide (marketing logo with tagline), consistent margins, page numbers.
- One idea per slide; charts use brand palette.

## Social conventions
- LinkedIn: fearless hook in line 1, insight body, soft CTA, 3–5 relevant hashtags.
- Carousels: 1 idea per slide, big readable headline (Helvetica Neue), consistent i3 template,
  blue/dark-blue base with a single expression-color accent.
