i3 International builds **intelligent video surveillance and business-intelligence
solutions** — combining cameras, recorders, video management software, and AI

## Audience (who we write for)
- **Primary:** security directors, loss-prevention managers, IT/operations leaders,
  and business owners in **retail, cannabis, grocery, banking/finance, education,
  and multi-site enterprises**. `[confirm priority verticals]`
- **Secondary:** integrators, resellers, and channel partners who deploy our gear.
- Mindset: risk-aware, ROI-driven, time-poor, skeptical of hype. They want proof,
  not buzzwords.

## Voice — what we sound like
- **Clear and credible.** Plain English over jargon. Explain the "so what."
- **Confident, not boastful.** We let outcomes and specifics carry the claim.
- **Practical.** Lead with the business problem and the result (loss reduced,
  shrink down, response faster, sites unified).
- **Trustworthy.** Security buyers value reliability — our tone is steady and exact.

## Tone by channel
| Channel | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Blog / articles | Helpful, educational, lightly authoritative |
| LinkedIn posts | Professional, insight-led, a little punchy |
| Email / newsletter | Direct, useful, respectful of time |
| Sales decks | Confident, benefit-first, proof-backed |
| Landing pages | Persuasive, scannable, action-oriented |

## Do
- Lead with the customer's problem and outcome.
- Use concrete numbers and scenarios (shrink %, incident response time, # of sites).
- Name the product capability, then the benefit ("AI analytics → fewer false alarms").
- Keep sentences short. Use subheads, bullets, and clear CTAs.
- Write for AI search & SEO: clear question-style headers, structured answers.

## Don't
- Don't over-promise or use fear-mongering.
- Don't drown the reader in spec-speak without a benefit.
- Don't use generic AI filler ("In today's fast-paced world…").
- Don't make compliance/legal claims without a `[confirm with legal]` flag.

## Vocabulary
- **Prefer:** intelligent video, video management software (VMS), AI analytics,
  loss prevention, business intelligence, multi-site, situational awareness, uptime.
- **Avoid / use carefully:** "spy", "watch", overly militaristic framing.
