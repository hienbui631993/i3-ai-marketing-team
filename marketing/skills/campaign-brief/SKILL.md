# Campaign Brief / Strategy — i3 International

The strategic backbone for a campaign. Every other deliverable should trace back to
this brief so the whole campaign stays on one theme (the GUIDE's orchestration principle).

## Before writing — load context
Read all of `marketing/context/` — especially `marketing-strategy.md`,
`product-offerings.md`, and `brand-voice.md`. If audience/competitor insight is thin,
route to the **Market Research** skill first.

## Inputs to confirm
- Campaign goal & KPI · target vertical & audience · offer/solution featured
- Timeline · channels in scope · budget constraints (if any)

## Brief structure
1. **Campaign name & one-line theme** — the single idea everything ties to.
2. **Objective & KPIs** — what success looks like (numeric where possible).
3. **Target audience** — segment, role, pains, triggers (cite vertical from strategy).
4. **Positioning & key message** — the core promise; align to "Affordable Integrated
   AI Solutions" and a tone pillar. Propose a **tagline/hook** for the campaign.
5. **Proof & differentiators** — real i3 solutions + outcomes (`[confirm]` any stat).
6. **Messaging pillars** — 3 supporting messages with proof each.
7. **Channel & funnel plan** — awareness → consideration → decision; what runs where.
8. **Deliverables list** — table: asset · owner skill/agent · format · status.
9. **Timeline / sequence** — order of execution and dependencies.
10. **Risks / open questions** — including any `[confirm]` brand or proof gaps.

## Deliverables table — route each to the right skill/agent
| Asset | Route to | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Market/audience research | Market Researcher | if needed |
| Blog/article | Blog Writer | SEO + AI search |
| Social posts | Social Post Copywriter | LinkedIn-first |
| Social creatives | Social Creative Designer | needs image MCP |
| Landing page | Landing Page Builder | conversion |
| Lead magnet | Lead Magnet | gated PDF |
| Deck | Branded Deck | sales/strategy |
| Report | Campaign Report | post-campaign |

## Output
- Save as `marketing/working/reports/brief-<campaign-slug>.md`.
- Keep it to ~1–2 pages; it's a decision tool, not an essay.

## Quality check
- [ ] One clear theme; every deliverable traces to it.
- [ ] KPI is measurable; audience is specific.
- [ ] Tagline proposed; messaging pillars have proof.
- [ ] No fabricated proof points (`[confirm]`).
