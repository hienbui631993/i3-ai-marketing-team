# Keyword Research — i3 International

Produce a practical keyword map that feeds the Blog Writer, Landing Page, and
Campaign Brief skills.

## Before starting — load context
Read `marketing/context/product-offerings.md` (real solution names) and
`marketing-strategy.md` (target verticals & funnel). Ground keywords in real i3
solutions (i3Ai Sentry, Heat Mapping, Smart-ER, Velocity Drive-thru Timer, etc.)
and verticals (Retail/LP, QSR, Schools).

## Inputs to confirm
- Topic / product / vertical · target funnel stage · any seed keywords
- Geography (if relevant) · whether live web data is available (WebSearch) or
  this is a structured-brainstorm pass.

## Method
1. **Seed list** — from the topic + i3 solution names + buyer language.
2. **Expand** — variations, questions ("how/what/why…"), comparisons, "near me"/vertical
   modifiers. If WebSearch is available, validate real phrasing and surface
   People-Also-Ask style questions; otherwise brainstorm and mark `[verify volume]`.
3. **Cluster** by intent: **Informational** (blog/AI-search), **Commercial**
   (comparison/solution), **Transactional** (demo/pricing/contact).
4. **Map to funnel & asset:** which cluster → which deliverable.
5. **Prioritize** — relevance × intent × (estimated) winnability. Don't invent exact
   volumes; flag `[verify volume]` where unknown.

## Output (`marketing/working/reports/keywords-<topic>.md`)
- Primary keyword + 5–15 secondary, grouped by intent cluster.
- A table: keyword · intent · funnel stage · suggested asset · notes.
- 5–10 **question headers** ready to drop into Blog Writer (AI-search optimized).
- Quick "content angle" recommendation.

## Quality check
- [ ] Tied to real i3 solutions & verticals.
- [ ] Clustered by intent; mapped to assets.
- [ ] No fabricated volume numbers (`[verify volume]` where unknown).
