# Social Post Copywriter — i3 International

Write scroll-stopping, on-brand social copy — primarily **LinkedIn** (B2B), with
optional adaptations for other platforms.

## Before writing — load context
Read `marketing/context/brand-voice.md`, `product-offerings.md`, and
`marketing-strategy.md`. Match the tone pillars (Inventive, Trustworthy, Fearless, Driven).

## Inputs to confirm
- Goal (awareness, lead-gen, event, product, thought leadership)
- Source material (a blog, campaign theme, stat, customer story) if repurposing
- Target vertical & the one CTA · platform(s) · how many posts

## LinkedIn post formula
1. **Hook (line 1)** — a fearless, specific opener that earns the click-to-expand.
   No "In today's world…". Lead with a number, a tension, or a bold claim.
2. **Insight body** — 2–5 short lines / mini-bullets. One idea. Concrete and useful.
   Reference real i3 solutions and outcomes (no fabricated stats — `[confirm]`).
3. **Soft CTA** — comment prompt, link, or "DM us" — one only.
4. **Hashtags** — 3–5 relevant (#LossPrevention #RetailAI #VideoAnalytics #QSR etc.).
5. Keep it skimmable; whitespace between lines.

## Other platforms (when asked)
- **X/Twitter:** tighter, punchier, 1–2 hashtags.
- **Instagram/Facebook:** warmer, pairs with a creative (route visuals to Social Creative Designer).
- **Carousel script:** if a carousel is wanted, output 1 idea per slide (headline +
  1-line body), then hand off to the **Social Creative Designer** skill for visuals.

## Output
- Save as `marketing/working/content/social-<topic>-<platform>.md`.
- For a set, number the posts and note suggested posting order/cadence.
- Include a one-line image/creative suggestion per post for the design hand-off.

## Quality check
- [ ] Hook works as a standalone first line.
- [ ] On-brand voice; one CTA; 3–5 hashtags (LinkedIn).
- [ ] Real solution names; no invented proof (`[confirm]` where unsure).
