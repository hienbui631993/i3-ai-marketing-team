# Research Brief — Retail Shrink & Affordable AI Loss Prevention

## Answer up front (the "so what")
Retail shrink is a large, rising, board-level cost, but most mid-market retailers
still investigate it **reactively** — pulling video after the loss. i3's wedge is
**affordable, integrated AI** that flags exceptions and anomalies *as they happen*
(i3Ai Smart-ER + Sentry) and explains *why* stores lose (Heat Mapping), across every
site from one platform. The campaign should sell **proactive, affordable** LP to
loss-prevention and operations leaders who feel priced out of "enterprise AI."

## Audience profile
- **Primary buyer:** Loss Prevention / Asset Protection Manager or Director at a
  multi-site retailer (grocery, convenience, specialty, cannabis).
- **Influencers:** Operations/Store Ops leaders, IT, Finance (signs off on cost).
- **Pains:**
  - Shrink eating margin; can't tell theft from process/error loss.
  - LP team is small and stretched across many sites — reactive, manual video review.
  - "AI loss prevention" sounds expensive and complex; fear of rip-and-replace.
  - Hard to prove ROI to Finance.
- **Triggers:** annual shrink results, a high-loss store, a tech refresh, new locations.
- **Objections:** cost, reliability, "is the AI real?", integration with existing cameras.

## Market context `[confirm with current sources]`
- Retail shrink is consistently cited by industry bodies as a multi-tens-of-billions
  problem and a top retail concern; a large share is internal/process, not just external
  theft. `[confirm with latest NRF / retail shrink survey figures before publishing]`
- Buying shift: from passive recording → **AI analytics + exception reporting**.
- Mid-market is under-served by enterprise-priced platforms → i3's "affordable" angle lands.

## Competitor snapshot
- **Enterprise VMS/analytics vendors:** powerful but expensive/complex; intimidating to mid-market.
- **Commodity camera/DVR vendors:** cheap, but passive — no real AI, no exception reporting.
- **i3's wedge:** *affordable + integrated + genuinely AI* — proactive exception reporting and
  business analytics without the enterprise price or rip-and-replace.

## Messaging implications
- Lead with **proactive vs. reactive** ("see shrink as it happens, not after").
- Hammer **affordable** + **no rip-and-replace / works across sites**.
- Make the AI **concrete**: Smart-ER (POS exceptions), Sentry (real-time alerts),
  Heat Mapping (why loss happens), Alert Center (one place to triage).
- Reframe ROI for Finance: shrink reduced > cost of platform.
- Tone: Fearless ("stop shrink") + Trustworthy ("decades of results") + warm/affordable.
