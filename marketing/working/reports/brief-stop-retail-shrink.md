# Campaign Brief — "Stop Shrink Before It Walks Out"

## Theme (one idea everything ties to)
**Proactive, affordable AI loss prevention** — see and stop shrink *as it happens*,
across every store, without an enterprise price tag.

## Objective & KPI
- **Objective:** generate qualified demo requests from multi-site retail LP/ops leaders.
- **Primary KPI:** demo requests from the landing page. `[set target with i3]`
- **Secondary:** blog traffic on shrink keywords, LinkedIn engagement, lead-magnet opt-ins.

## Target audience
Loss Prevention / Asset Protection managers & directors at mid-market multi-site
retailers (grocery, convenience, specialty, cannabis); influenced by Ops, IT, Finance.
Pains: rising shrink, stretched reactive LP teams, fear that "AI LP" = expensive/complex.
(See `reports/research-retail-shrink.md`.)

## Positioning & key message
i3 = **Affordable Integrated AI Solutions.** For retail LP: *"You shouldn't have to
choose between enterprise-grade AI and a budget you can defend. i3 catches shrink as it
happens — across every site — at a price that pays for itself."*

- **Campaign tagline:** **"Stop Shrink Before It Walks Out."**
- **Tone pillars:** Fearless (stop it) + Trustworthy (decades of results) + Affordable/warm.

## Proof & differentiators
- **Proactive, not reactive:** real-time exception/anomaly alerts vs. after-the-fact review.
- **Affordable + integrated:** AI + cloud across all sites; no rip-and-replace.
- **Concrete AI:** i3Ai **Smart-ER** (POS exceptions), **Sentry** (real-time AI alerts),
  **Heat Mapping** (why loss happens), **Alert Center** (one place to triage).
- *(All customer stats `[confirm / gather]` before publishing.)*

## Messaging pillars
1. **See it as it happens** — Smart-ER + Sentry surface exceptions in real time. *(proof: `[confirm]`)*
2. **Understand why** — Heat Mapping + analytics separate theft from process loss. *(proof: `[confirm]`)*
3. **Afford it everywhere** — one affordable, cloud-based platform across all sites. *(proof: `[confirm]`)*
